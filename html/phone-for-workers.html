iPhone 13 Pro Max

Apple has refreshed its iPhone Pro lineup this year with the iPhone 13 Pro and iPhone 13 Pro Max. Both the iPhone 13 Pro models feature identical specs save for the display and offer new, improved cameras with OIS, 4K 60 ProRes video, Cinematic Mode, 120 Hz ProMotion display, A15 Bionic SoC, and up to 1 TB storage.

Apple's latest and greatest smartphones are all the buzz after they have officially launched last week, and profound reviews of the flagships of the Cupertino-based company are slowly but surely appearing online. Among others, the tech website DisplayMate has posted its review of the display in the new iPhone, and apparently the OLED panel that is built into the iPhone 13 Pro Max (from US$1,099 on Amazon) has proven to be particularly impressive.

The Super Retina XDR display with the 120Hz ProMotion feature was able to set several records. It is for example the brightest OLED smartphone on the market with a peak fullscreen brightness of over 1,000 nits. When watching HDR content, the display can even reach 1,200 nits. As every OLED display, contrasts are absolutely flawless, but DisplayMate also praises the impeccable color accuracy of the Apple smartphone.

Features

  • Processor: Apple A15 Bionic
  • Graphics adapter: Apple A15 Bionic
  • Memory: 8192 MB
  • Display: 6.7 inch 19.5:9, 120Hz
  • Storage: 1 TB
  • Price: 1099 Euro

Samsung Galaxy S21+

More high-end. Samsung's Galaxy S21+ flagship smartphone offers all the equipment characteristics that also distinguish the excellent Galaxy S21. But the plus sign in the product label also means that you can expect even more here. And indeed, the Galaxy S21+ delivers: for an additional 200 Euros (~$242), you get a significantly larger display and a noticeably larger battery, among other things.

The flagship smartphones of the Galaxy S21 series attract a wide group of buyers. As its most affordable representative, the 6.2-inch Galaxy S21 will probably win the most takers. However, there is also the 6.8-inch Galaxy S21 Ultra, the flagship of the series with the best camera. In between the two is the Galaxy S21+, which also chose the golden middle path in terms of the equipment.

With its large, 6.7-inch display, the Galaxy S21+ is supposed to particularly attract gamers and fans of the series, Samsung says. Correspondingly with its 4800-mAh battery, the smartphone offers the second largest battery of the Galaxy S21 series, and like its siblings also uses a bright AMOLED display and the fast Exynos 2100.

Features

  • Processor: Samsung Exynos 2100 5G 8 x 2.2 - 2.9 GHz, Exynos X1 / Cortex-A78 / A55
  • Graphics adapter: ARM Mali-G78 MP14
  • Memory: 8192 MB
  • Display: 6.70 inch 20:9, 2400 x 1080 pixel 393 PPI
  • Storage: 128 GB UFS 3.1 Flash
  • Price: 1049 Euro

Huawei Mate 40 Pro

The Huawei Mate 40 Pro turned out to be a powerful high-end smartphone in the test, shining with many premium features. The biggest shortcoming in Germany is the lack of Google services. But are they really absolutely necessary? And what alternatives are there?

The Huawei Mate 40 series reappears with four models: The Mate 40, Mate 40 Pro, Mate 40 Pro+, and Mate 40 RS. Only the Pro version is coming to Europe this year, and this speaks volumes about the sales figures Huawei expects, which are due to the involuntary absence of Google services.

The Huawei Mate 40 Pro is powered by the Kirin 9000, which is particularly supposed to outperform the Snapdragon 865+ in all performance aspects. In addition, the Chinese provide 8 GB of RAM and 256 GB of internal storage; the latter can be expanded with a nano memory card. No compromises have to be made when it comes to connectivity either. Huawei promises the fastest 5G, Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.2, and USB 3.2. Whether with or without a cable, the Mate 40 Pro offers the latest data transfer technologies. The new Leica triple camera is also expected to set new standards.

Features

  • Processor: HiSilicon Kirin 9000 8 x 2.1 - 3.1 GHz, Cortex-A77/-A55
  • Graphics adapter: ARM Mali-G78 MP24
  • Memory: 8192 MB
  • Display: 6.76 inch 18.56:9, 2772 x 1344 pixel 456 PPI
  • Storage: 256 GB UFS 3.1 Flash
  • Price: 1,199 Euro
